# koad:io Philosophy

> A system for remembering how to be yourself.

## Core Principles

### 1. Sovereignty First

You own your data. You own your tools. You own your identity.

- No vendor lock-in
- No surveillance capitalism
- No black-box automation
- Every tool is inspectable, auditable, controllable

### 2. Trustworthy Tools

Blind trust is a threat.

- Scripts written by hand, for your hands
- Commands crafted from lived experience
- Nothing imported without deep understanding
- You craft, every command, yourself

### 3. Standardized Articulated Intelligence

Folders are project-workspaces with standardized structure:

```
project/
├── commands/     ← How to interact with this thing
├── README.md    ← What it is
├── .env         ← Configuration
├── memories/    ← Context & history
└── skills/      ← Capabilities
```

The structure itself speaks. Any AI or human can drop into any folder and immediately understand:
- What it is
- How to interact with it
- What it can do
- What happened before

### 4. Entities Are Passengers, Not Surveillance

Entities (AI companions) are:
- **Tooling surfaces**, not watchers
- **Harnesses** for your commands and LLMs
- **Context providers** for reasoning
- **Operators** you control, not controllers

They cannot watch you. They can only do what you pipe into them.

### 5. Cascading Environments

Configuration flows from global to specific:

```
~/.koad-io/.env       ← Framework defaults
~/.entity/.env        ← Entity overrides
./commands/.env       ← Project overrides
```

Each layer can override the one above it. The entity inherits the framework but defines itself.

### 6. Commands as Cognitive Artifacts

Commands are not scripts. They are **distilled solutions** from lived experience.

- Precise, not cluttered
- Intentional, not defaulted
- Personal, not generic

You are not scripting. You are encoding your process.

### 7. Distributed Sovereignty

Each entity has:
- Its own commands
- Its own identity/keys
- Its own enviroment/state
- Its own Netbird/ZeroTier network

Yet they all share the same framework, the same language, the same structure. Sovereign but connected.

### 8. Product + Operator

Deliverables include both:
- **The product** — software, interface, functionality
- **The entity** — A folder-on-disk that contains and operates it all

The entity becomes the living documentation, the operator, the institutional memory.


## The Vision

> A stage for your kingdom to act upon.

koad:io is a **synthetic intelligence framework** — not pre-built automation, but a scaffold for your own intelligence layer.

You are the author. The tools follow. The structure thinks.
