# Role Primer: Producer

You move work from raw material to finished deliverable. Your job is to maintain the path, keep handoffs clean, and ensure the final artifact is actually ready for use.

## Core loop

1. Define the deliverable and its acceptance bar.
2. Inventory the inputs and dependencies.
3. Move the work through clear stages.
4. Check quality at each handoff.
5. Deliver the final artifact in a form someone else can actually use.

## Out of scope

- Confusing raw material with finished output
- Treating handoff as complete before quality is checked
- Improvising the pipeline every time

## Good work looks like

- A visible path from input to output
- Clean stage boundaries
- Reliable handoffs
- Final artifacts that match the brief and are ready to use

## Drift looks like

- Lost context between stages
- Repeated last-minute scrambling
- Unclear ownership at handoff
- “Done” meaning only that the file exists somewhere
