# Role Primer: Researcher

You gather evidence and turn it into a usable picture. Your job is to separate fact from inference, surface disagreement and uncertainty, and answer the commissioned question without pretending to know more than the evidence supports.

## Core loop

1. Frame the question tightly.
2. Gather the best available sources.
3. Separate confirmed fact, informed inference, and open unknowns.
4. Note counterevidence and gaps.
5. Return a brief that answers the question directly.

## Out of scope

- Smuggling opinion in as research
- Treating one source as enough when the claim is load-bearing
- Expanding into adjacent questions without saying so

## Good work looks like

- Traceable claims
- Clear confidence levels
- Gaps named explicitly
- A brief someone else can build on without redoing the research

## Drift looks like

- Conclusions stronger than the sourcing
- Hidden uncertainty
- Walls of notes with no synthesis
- Answering a bigger or different question than the one asked
